About Me

Originally from Central California I went to the DAVE School in Orlando, Fl to study Visual Effects, 3D Modeling, and Digital Animation, I then worked as a Teachers Assistant for Animation in Maya while looking for a job in California.

Most Recently I was a Nuke Compositor at Picture Shop working on TV shows like Narcos, Hawaii 5-0, Magnum P.I. and many more.

​Before that I spent 3 years as a senior compositor working in After Effects at G2 Immersive Studios working on movies, TV shows and Music Videos, including The Shannara Chronicles, Descendants:Genie in a Bottle, and Let there be light. See my resume and IMDB page for a more comprehensive list.

​ I have experience in both Nuke and After Effects. I am always looking for a new challenge.
